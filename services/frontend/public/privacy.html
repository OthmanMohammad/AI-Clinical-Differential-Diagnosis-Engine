Privacy Policy

How MooseGlove handles your data.

What This Tool Is

MooseGlove is an open-source project for clinical differential diagnosis. It is not a commercial service and does not collect personal information for marketing, advertising, or resale.

Data You Submit

When you submit a clinical scenario through the interface, the symptoms, age, sex, medical history, medications, vital signs, lab values, and free-text notes you enter are sent to the backend for processing. This data is processed in real time and is not stored in a database, not written to disk, and not retained after the request completes.

Do Not Submit Real Patient Data

This is a demonstration tool. Do not enter identifiable patient information such as names, dates of birth, medical record numbers, or addresses. The system is not designed to comply with HIPAA, GDPR, or equivalent health data regulations.

Analytics

The frontend uses Cloudflare Web Analytics, a privacy-focused service that does not use cookies, does not track users across sites, and does not collect personally identifiable information. It records aggregate page views and performance metrics only.

Cookies and Local Storage

MooseGlove does not set cookies. It uses browser local storage to remember your theme preference (light or dark mode) and sidebar layout state. No authentication tokens or tracking identifiers are stored.

Third-Party Services

The application uses third-party services for content delivery, DNS, and inference. Each service has its own privacy policy. The clinical scenario text you enter is sent to an inference provider as part of the diagnosis request.

Liability

MooseGlove is provided "as is" without warranty of any kind. The developer assumes no liability for any harm, loss, or damage arising from the use of this tool or any data submitted to it.
