Medical Disclaimer

Not a Medical Device

MooseGlove is an educational portfolio project that demonstrates AI-assisted clinical differential diagnosis. It is not a medical device, not FDA-cleared, and not CE-marked. It has not been validated in a clinical setting, peer-reviewed, or approved by any regulatory authority.

No Doctor-Patient Relationship

Using MooseGlove does not create a doctor-patient relationship. The output does not constitute medical advice, a diagnosis, or a treatment recommendation. Do not rely on this tool for any real clinical decision.

For Demonstration Purposes Only

The system retrieves information from a biomedical knowledge graph (PrimeKG) and uses large language models to generate ranked differential diagnoses. Both the knowledge graph and the language models can produce incomplete, inaccurate, or misleading results. The output should be treated as a technology demonstration, not as clinically actionable information.

Emergency Situations

If you are experiencing a medical emergency, call your local emergency number (911 in the United States) or go to the nearest emergency department immediately. Do not use this tool to evaluate emergency symptoms.

Limitations

The underlying knowledge graph has known gaps in phenotype coverage for common diseases. The language model may produce hallucinated citations that appear grounded but are not supported by the retrieved evidence. Confidence scores are model-generated estimates, not calibrated probabilities. Rare diseases may be overrepresented in results due to the structure of the knowledge graph.
